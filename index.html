Rules to never break

How Markets Actually Work
Understand price action, candlesticks, and how buyers & sellers drive movement. No trading — just observing.
📚 What to study
  • What is price action? Buyers vs sellers, bid/ask spread
  • How candlesticks form and what they represent
  • Difference between trending and ranging markets
  • Babypips — School of Pipsology, chapters 1–3

  • Set up free TradingView account
  • Load EUR/USD chart and just watch daily
  • Spend 1–2 hrs observing — zero trades
  • Write at least one observation in your journal

02
Market Structure & Key Levels
Learn to identify where price has been and where it's likely to react. Support, resistance, and trend structure.
📚 What to study
  • Higher highs / higher lows — bullish structure
  • Lower highs / lower lows — bearish structure
  • How to draw support & resistance correctly
  • Multiple timeframe analysis (1D → 4H → 1H)
  • YouTube: "market structure for beginners" (2–3 videos)
🛠 Daily tasks
  • Mark S&R levels on EUR/USD each morning
  • Identify trend direction on 3 timeframes
  • Screenshot + annotate at least one chart daily
  • Note if price respected any of your levels
🎯 Weekly goals
Draw S&R on a live chart confidently without second-guessing
Identify bullish vs bearish market structure on any chart
Have 5+ journal entries with annotated screenshots

Week 2 — key notes & observations
03
Risk Management
The most important week of the month. This is what separates traders who survive from those who blow accounts.
⚠️
Do not skip or rush this week. More traders fail from poor risk management than from bad analysis. If you only master one thing this month, make it this. Every concept here applies for the rest of your trading career.
📚 What to study
  • Risk/reward ratio — what 1:2 and 1:3 means
  • Position sizing — how to calculate lot size
  • The 1–2% rule per trade — non-negotiable
  • Stop loss placement — logic, not random
  • Practice with a forex position size calculator
🛠 Daily tasks
  • Start paper trading on TradingView (2–3 trades/day)
  • Calculate position size BEFORE every single trade
  • Log every paper trade in the table below
  • Review trades at end of day — why did they work/fail?
🎯 Weekly goals
Calculate correct position size before every paper trade
Never enter a trade without a pre-defined stop loss
Achieve minimum 1:2 risk/reward on all planned trades

04
Build Your First Strategy
Combine everything from weeks 1–3 into one repeatable, rule-based setup you can test consistently.
📚 What to study
  • How to combine trend + key level + risk management
  • What makes a strategy "valid" — rules over feelings
  • Re-watch week 1–2 videos with fresh eyes
  • Review your week 3 trade log for patterns
🛠 Daily tasks
  • Only enter trades that match your written rules
  • Paper trade 2–3 quality setups per day
  • After each trade: did I follow my rules? Yes/No
  • Full monthly review on Day 28
🎯 Weekly goals
Write your strategy rules clearly in plain language (1 page max)
Complete a full month of consistent journal entries
Review paper trading results and assess honestly
